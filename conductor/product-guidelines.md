# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Concise & Efficient**: Communication with users will prioritize clarity and speed. Information will be presented using short sentences and bullet points to facilitate quick reading and comprehension by artists on the go.

## Visual Branding
- **Minimalist & Neutral**: The visual identity will be clean and understated. This "white-cube" inspired aesthetic ensures that the content—the grant information and potentially artist work—takes center stage without visual distraction.

## UX Principles
- **Accessibility First**: The application will be designed to be inclusive and accessible to all users, adhering to modern accessibility standards for screen readers and keyboard navigation.
- **Mobile-First Responsiveness**: Designing with a mobile-first approach ensures that the application provides a seamless experience across all devices, particularly for artists who may access information while away from their studios.

## Navigation and Hierarchy
- **Flat & Simple**: The application's structure will favor a flat hierarchy with a few clear, top-level categories. This approach minimizes the number of clicks required to find information and ensures a straightforward exploration experience.
