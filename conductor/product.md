# Product Guide

## Project Overview
An application for organizing information on contemporary art grants and competitions.

## Target Users
- **Artists**: Individual artists looking for funding and competition opportunities to support their practice.

## Key Features
- **Search & Filter**: Comprehensive search functionality to filter opportunities by category, deadline, or region.
- **Deadline Alerts**: Automated notifications (email or push) to inform users of upcoming application deadlines.
- **Save/Bookmark**: Ability for users to save and track grants and competitions to their profiles for later review.

## Platform
- **Web Application**: A browser-accessible application to ensure wide availability and ease of access on various devices.

## Functional Goals
- **Data Accuracy**: Maintain a high level of reliability and currency in the listings through robust verification and update processes.
- **Ease of Use**: Provide a simple, clean, and intuitive interface that allows artists to find and manage information efficiently.
